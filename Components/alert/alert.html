Alerts

Alerts are used to force the user to read a specific message which might specify danger,success,warning orcertain information to the user. It takes away the attention of the user from current window and diverts him to specific message.

Success! Indicates positive action to the user. close
Danger! Indicates danger negative action to the user. close
Warning Alert. Indicates warning to the user. close
Primary Alert. Indicates any regular or normal information to the user. close
Secondary Alert. Indicates any secondary information to the user. close
